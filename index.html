What is SWARM?

Working with Welsh Government and NHS Wales, SWARM is The South Wales Additive and Rapid Manufacturing Consortia. It is here to help the NHS source and supply items it critically needs to address Covid-19.

SWARM is an initiative to help industry meet the demands of the primary supply chain and to pass intelligence to the NHS about materials and ideas industry, manufacturing and design might have, that could help their efforts.
